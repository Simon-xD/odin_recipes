Pasta

Description

Pasta with tomato sauce is an extremely simple dish. It tastes great and is easy to make, ideal for students or beginners.

Estimated cooking time: 15 min

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Your favorite pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Various spices
  • Bacon
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water
  2. Fry the bacon and sliced onions
  3. Add the garlic
  4. Cut the tomatoes and put them in the pan
  5. Let it cook for a while and spice it to your liking
  6. Bon appetit!
